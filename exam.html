EXAMINATION

The CSE department will schedule exams in alignment with the academic calendar, ensuring minimal overlap with other departmental exams. Subject-specific examination slots will be allocated to cater to the unique requirements of CSE courses. Answer scripts for CSE subjects will be evaluated by faculty with relevant expertise to ensure accurate assessment of technical knowledge and skills. Digital tools specific to coding and technical problem-solving will be employed during evaluation to maintain consistency and accuracy. Results for CSE exams will be published within a predetermined timeline to ensure timely feedback and progression tracking for students. Certificates and grade sheets will highlight key competencies and skills relevant to the CSE field.
  • CSE students will receive detailed briefings on academic and assessment evaluation processes
  • The CSE department will regularly review and update examination practices based on feedback from students, faculty, and industry stakeholders.
  • During the induction program, including guidelines on software and tools used for exams and submissions.
  • Regular orientation sessions on examination rules and regulations will be conducted with a focus on CSE-specific guidelines
  • A dedicated helpdesk will be established within the CSE department to address examination-related queries and issues promptly.
  • Innovative assessment methods, such as project-based evaluations and hackathons, will be integrated to provide a holistic assessment of students' skills.
